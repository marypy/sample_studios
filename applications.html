Graduate Studio Residency Award

Each year Sample-Studios, Cork, offers a studio residency award to graduates of Crawford College of Art and Design, Cork and Limerick School of Art and Design. This graduate award offers:

• a 6-month free membership of the studios
• full use of studio facilities and workspaces
• support from Sample-Studios staff and the TACTIC curatorial team.

The residency will culminate in the exhibition of new work in collaboration with TACTIC and the full support of Sample-Studios. Residencies will commence in late 2018, with the date to be arranged to suit the recipient. The successful applicant will be presented with the award following a visit to their degree show by the curatorial team.

Past recipients of this award include: Roisín McCabe (LSAD), Chloe Tetrault Kearney (CCAD), Sophie Gough (LSAD), Evgeniya Martirosyan (CCAD), Andrew McSweeney (CCAD), David Mathúna (CCAD), John Dwyer (CCAD), Robert Power (CCAD).

Graduate Curatorial Residency Award

Sample-Studios are pleased to invite applications for our Graduate Curatorial Residency Award 2018/2019. This award offers:

• a free 6-month membership of the studios
• full use of studio facilities and workspaces
• support from Sample-Studios staff and the TACTIC curatorial team.

The graduate will be awarded a fully-funded online course in curatorial studies from the NODE Centre for Curatorial Studies (http://www.nodecenter.org), after which they will have the opportunity to curate the Sample-Studios Annual Members’ Exhibition. The graduate will also have the opportunity to curate their own exhibition with the support of the curatorial team as part of the 2018 TACTIC programme.

The successful applicant will be offered training in front of house, event management and planning, artist liaison, gallery management and administration. The graduate will be a key part of the TACTIC team in the planning and implementation of our Visual Art Programme. Residencies will commence in late 2017 at a date to suit the recipient. Successful applicants will be presented with the award following a visit to their degree show by the curatorial team.

How to apply

To apply for either, or both, of the above opportunities, send the below information, along with your CV, to:

info@sample-studios.com

Please include either Graduate Studio Residency Award or Graduate Curatorial Residency Award in the subject heading. Applications are accepted from 1 May 2018 up to 6 June 2018.

Important information

• The TACTIC and Sample-Studios team will visit both the LSAD and CCAD degree shows in June 2018.

• To be considered for for either awards, your interest must be registered by 6 June. Awards will be granted based on both the merit of the application and the work on display at the respective graduate exhibitions.

• Applicants should be willing to take up their residency before October 2018.

• Should an applicant have other obligations or opportunities, this will be taken into consideration and the residency may be postponed/expedited.

• Due to the limited resources of our artist-run organisation, we are unfortunately not in a position to visit degree shows outside of Limerick (LSAD) and Cork (CCAD) in order to consider applicants outside of these colleges. We do however have a number of different membership options for new graduates which you are welcome to apply for at info@sample-studios.com.
